More Water For Kenya

The introduction of new water sources, including wells, sand dams, spring protections, and rain tanks, aims to address the critical water situation in Kenya.
Kenya, with its diverse landscape of cities, countryside, mountains, and desert, possesses great potential amid challenging poverty.
Despite the inspiring hospitality, kindness, and joy of its people, many in Kenya face the harsh reality of not having access to clean and safe water.
Mothers tirelessly search for it, and children resort to collecting dirty water when no other options are available, leading to a cycle of despair with inevitable health issues and increased poverty.
